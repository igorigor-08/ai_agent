### **Loan Portfolio** – _Кредитный портфель_  
The total outstanding loans issued by a bank, covering both corporate and retail segments. The portfolio is analyzed to assess risk, profitability, and capital requirements.  

### **Corporate Lending** – _Корпоративное кредитование_  
Loans provided to businesses and corporations, often larger in size and requiring detailed credit analysis.

### **Retail Lending** – _Розничное кредитование_  
Loans issued to individual consumers, including mortgages, auto loans, and personal loans.

### **Credit Risk** – _Кредитный риск_  
The risk that a borrower will default on their debt obligations, leading to financial loss for the lender.

### **Default** – _Дефолт_  
The failure of a borrower to meet debt obligations, typically defined as being **90+ days past due**.

### **Exposure at Default (EAD)** – _Объем экспозиции на момент дефолта (EAD)_  
The total value a bank is exposed to if a borrower defaults.  
\[
EAD = \text{Outstanding Loan Balance} + \text{Undrawn Credit Commitments} \times \text{Credit Conversion Factor (CCF)}
\]

### **Loss Given Default (LGD)** – _Убыток при дефолте (LGD)_  
The proportion of total exposure that will be lost if a borrower defaults.  
\[
LGD = 1 - \text{Recovery Rate}
\]

### **Probability of Default (PD)** – _Вероятность дефолта (PD)_  
The likelihood that a borrower will default within a given time frame, usually **one year**.

### **Expected Loss (EL)** – _Ожидаемый убыток (EL)_  
The average loss expected due to defaults over a period.  
\[
EL = EAD \times PD \times LGD
\]

### **Unexpected Loss (UL)** – _Неожиданный убыток (UL)_  
The deviation from expected loss, used for capital calculations.  
\[
UL = \sqrt{\text{Variance}(EL)}
\]

## **Risk Metrics & Capital Requirements**
### **Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA)** – _Активы, взвешенные по риску (RWA)_  
Assets adjusted for risk based on regulatory guidelines (Basel).  
\[
RWA = \sum (\text{Asset Exposure} \times \text{Risk Weight})
\]

### **Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR)** – _Коэффициент достаточности капитала (CAR)_  
A measure of a bank’s capital against its risk exposure.  
\[
CAR = \frac{\text{Tier 1 + Tier 2 Capital}}{RWA}
\]

### **Basel III** – _Базель III_  
A global regulatory framework for managing bank capital and liquidity to ensure stability.

### **Internal Ratings-Based Approach (IRB)** – _Подход внутреннего рейтинга (ПВР)_  
A risk management approach allowing banks to use internal models for capital calculations.

### **Stress Testing** – _Стресс-тестирование_  
Simulating adverse economic conditions to assess financial stability.

### **Economic Capital** – _Экономический капитал_  
The capital required to absorb unexpected losses and maintain solvency.

### **Regulatory Capital** – _Регуляторный капитал_  
The minimum capital banks must hold per regulatory requirements.

### **Expected Credit Loss (ECL)** – _Ожидаемые кредитные убытки (ECL)_  
The loss calculated under IFRS 9 for provisioning.  
\[
ECL = EAD \times PD \times LGD
\]

### **Collective Provisions** – _Групповые резервы_  
Reserves set aside for losses on a group of loans with similar risk characteristics.

### **Individual Provisions** – _Индивидуальные резервы_  
Reserves assigned for specific problem loans.

## **Loan Portfolio Performance Indicators**
### **Net Interest Margin (NIM)** – _Чистая процентная маржа (NIM)_  
The difference between interest earned and interest paid, relative to assets.  
\[
NIM = \frac{\text{Interest Income} - \text{Interest Expense}}{\text{Average Earning Assets}}
\]

### **Cost of Risk (CoR)** – _Стоимость риска (CoR)_  
Loan loss provisions as a percentage of total loans.  
\[
CoR = \frac{\text{Loan Loss Provisions}}{\text{Total Loans}}
\]

### **Non-Performing Loans (NPL)** – _Проблемные кредиты (NPL)_  
Loans that are **90+ days past due** or unlikely to be repaid.

### **NPL Ratio** – _Доля проблемных кредитов_  
The proportion of non-performing loans in the total loan portfolio.  
\[
NPL \ Ratio = \frac{\text{Non-Performing Loans}}{\text{Total Loans}}
\]

### **Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR)** – _Коэффициент кредитов к депозитам (LDR)_  
A measure of liquidity risk.  
\[
LDR = \frac{\text{Total Loans}}{\text{Total Deposits}}
\]

### **Charge-Off Rate** – _Коэффициент списания убытков_  
The percentage of loans written off as unrecoverable.  
\[
\text{Charge-Off Rate} = \frac{\text{Charge-Offs}}{\text{Total Loans}}
\]

### **Recovery Rate** – _Коэффициент возврата_  
The percentage of defaulted loans recovered.  
\[
\text{Recovery Rate} = \frac{\text{Recovered Amount}}{\text{Defaulted Loan Amount}}
\]

## **Credit Scoring & Underwriting**
### **Credit Score** – _Кредитный скоринг_  
A numerical representation of a borrower’s creditworthiness.

### **Loan-to-Value Ratio (LTV)** – _Коэффициент LTV (кредит к стоимости залога)_  
The ratio of loan amount to the appraised value of collateral.  
\[
LTV = \frac{\text{Loan Amount}}{\text{Collateral Value}}
\]

### **Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)** – _Коэффициент долговой нагрузки (DTI)_  
A borrower’s total monthly debt payments relative to income.  
\[
DTI = \frac{\text{Monthly Debt Payments}}{\text{Gross Monthly Income}}
\]

### **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)** – _Коэффициент покрытия долга (DSCR)_  
Used in corporate lending to assess ability to repay debt.  
\[
DSCR = \frac{\text{Net Operating Income}}{\text{Debt Service}}
\]

## **Portfolio Risk Analytics & Modeling**
### **Monte Carlo Simulation** – _Моделирование Монте-Карло_  
A risk analysis technique that simulates thousands of scenarios to estimate distributions.

### **Credit Migration Analysis** – _Анализ миграции кредитов_  
Tracking borrower credit rating changes over time.

### **Vintage Analysis** – _Винтаж-анализ_  
Comparing loan performance by origination period.

### **Transition Matrix** – _Матрица переходов_  
A table showing probability of credit rating changes.

## **Regulatory & Compliance**
### **IFRS 9** – _МСФО 9_  
Accounting standard requiring Expected Credit Loss (ECL) provisioning.

### **CECL (Current Expected Credit Loss)** – _Текущие ожидаемые кредитные убытки (CECL)_  
A US accounting standard similar to IFRS 9.

### **Risk Appetite Framework** – _Рамки риск-аппетита_  
Defines acceptable levels of risk a bank is willing to take.
